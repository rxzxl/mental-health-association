About Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Learn More

What is Mental Health?

Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood. It includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

Learn More

Types of Mental Health

There are 3 types of mental health:

Depression

"Depression is persistent sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in life.".

Anxiety

"Anxiety is excessive worry, fear, and restlessness that affect daily life.".
